ABOUT US

The Architectural Interior Design Firm “THE DESIGN ASPECT” Was Established In Mumbai In 2015 By Darshit Tank. Our Priorities When Creating Spaces Are The Following: Harmony With The Surrounding Environment, An Intuitive Approach To The Functions Of The Future Site, A Clarity For The Layout Plan, A Balanced And Consistent Aesthetic Awareness, And Exciting Visual Attractivity. To Achieve These Goals And Create Social Value, We Conduct Meetings With Our Clients And Research The Site. We Do Not Offer Designs Of Half-Baked Quality. We Are Extremely Particular About The Design And Refine It Until We Achieve A Quality That Satisfies You. We Carefully Examine Each Space And Propose New Possibilities By Designing And Selecting Appropriate Materials, Furniture, Fixtures, Lighting, And Artwork. We Operate In All Sectors Of Building Industry From Hotels, Showrooms, Health Care, Offices To Residences Architectural And Interior Design.

Darshit Believes That Space Create The Memories And Experiences Of The People Who Visit There. He Wants To Create A Space That Leaves Pleasant Memories And Experiences In People’s Hearts. His Mission Is To Design Spaces That Are Better, More Comfortable And Creative. He Always Strive To Provide New Values In Space.
